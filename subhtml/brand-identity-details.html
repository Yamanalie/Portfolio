How it works

The journey from brief to brand.

A structured process that keeps you informed at every stage — no surprises, just results.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a 30-minute call to understand your business, your audience, your competitors, and what makes you different. This shapes everything.

02

Research & Strategy

Deep market research, competitor analysis, and positioning work. I map out the visual territory your brand should own before touching any design tools.

03

Concept Development

I develop 2–3 distinct logo concepts with rationale for each. Every design decision is intentional and explained — not just aesthetic.

04

Refinement Rounds

You choose a direction and we refine. Included revision rounds let us dial in the details until it feels exactly right. No rushed decisions.

05

System Build & Delivery

Once the logo is locked, I build out the full system — typography, colours, patterns, mockups, and the brand guidelines document. Everything is organised and packaged for immediate use.

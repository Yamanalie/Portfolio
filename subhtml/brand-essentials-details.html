What's included

Five essentials. Nothing missing.

This package is designed to give you a professional, complete foundation — not a stripped-down starter. Everything included is essential. Everything excluded is optional.

01

Logo — All Formats

Your logo is the face of your brand. It's delivered in every format you'll ever need — so you're never scrambling when someone asks for 'the logo file.'

PNG (transparent background) — for digital use, presentations, documents
JPG (white & dark backgrounds) — for social media, email signatures
SVG — infinitely scalable vector for web developers
PDF — print-ready, high resolution for signage, packaging, print
Full colour, reversed, and single-colour variations included
02

Typography

The right typefaces transform your brand from generic to distinctive. I select and pair fonts that match your brand personality — and give you the rules for using them consistently.

Primary display typeface — for headlines, logos, key messaging
Secondary body typeface — for paragraphs, captions, supporting text
Usage rules — size hierarchy, spacing, dos and don'ts
Free font alternatives included where applicable
03

Colour Palette

Colour is the most immediately recognisable element of any brand. A well-built palette gives your team the exact colours to use — every time, on every platform.

Primary brand colour with full code set (HEX, RGB, CMYK, Pantone reference)
Secondary and accent colours with the same code sets
Neutral / background colours for both light and dark use
Contrast ratio notes — accessibility compliance guidance
04

Mockups

Seeing your logo on a business card, a phone screen, or a tote bag transforms how you and your clients feel about the brand. Mockups make it real before anything is printed.

3 photorealistic mockup scenes — selected based on your industry
Options include: business card, packaging, apparel, phone screen, signage, stationery
High resolution (min. 3000px) — print and presentation ready
Delivered as JPG and PNG — ready to share on social or in proposals
05

2 Revision Rounds

Good design is a conversation, not a one-shot delivery. Two structured revision rounds mean you get a result that actually feels right — not just 'good enough.'

Round 1: After initial concept presentation — directional feedback
Round 2: After refinement — detail and polish feedback
Clear feedback process — no vague 'make it pop' requests
Additional rounds available if needed at an agreed rate

How it works

Simple. Fast. Professional.

01

Brief & Intake

You fill in a short project brief — your business, your audience, your vibe preferences, and any references. No lengthy calls required at this stage.

02

Concept Presentation

Within the agreed timeline, I present 1–2 logo concepts with typography and colour direction. Each concept comes with a clear rationale — not just 'here are some options.'

03

Revision Rounds

You choose a direction and share structured feedback. I refine through 2 rounds until it feels exactly right. The feedback process is guided — no vague feedback loops.

04

Final Delivery

All files packaged and delivered via a shared folder — logo in every format, colour codes, typography guide, and mockups. Ready to use immediately.

01
Discovery

We start by understanding — deeply.

Before any design work begins, I need to understand your business, your audience, your goals, and your competitive landscape. You have two ways to do this — choose what works for you.

📞

Discovery Call

A 30-minute video or voice call where we talk through your project, your vision, and your questions. The most efficient way to get aligned fast.

✓ Best for complex projects
📋

Written Questionnaire

Prefer to work async? Fill in a structured project brief at your own pace. 10 targeted questions that give me everything I need — no call required.

✓ Best for clear-scope projects
⏱ Timeline: Day 1
02
Research

Research before pencil touches paper.

Most designers skip this step. I don't. I spend time mapping your market, studying your competitors, and defining the visual territory your brand should own — before any design tool opens.

Competitor visual audit — what's already in your space
Audience profile — who we're designing for and why
Brand positioning — where you sit, where you should sit
Moodboard — visual direction presented for your approval before design starts
⏱ Timeline: Days 2–4
03
Concept

Concepts — with reasons, not just options.

I develop 2–3 distinct logo directions — each with a clear rationale explaining the thinking behind every visual decision. You're not choosing between random options. You're choosing between deliberate strategies.

2–3 concept directions presented in a clean presentation format
Each concept shown in context — not just on a white background
Written rationale for every design choice — colour, form, typography
One direction is chosen — we move forward together from here
⏱ Timeline: Days 5–10
04
Refinement

Structured revision. Not endless back-and-forth.

Revision rounds are structured — not open-ended. Each round has a clear scope, a deadline, and a feedback format that keeps us both focused on making it better, not just different.

Round 1 — directional changes: layout, spacing, overall feel
Round 2 — detail polish: weight, colour adjustments, fine-tuning
Additional rounds available if needed at an agreed flat rate
Feedback is guided — I give you a structured template so nothing important gets missed
⏱ Timeline: Days 11–18
05
Build

From logo to full system.

Once the logo is locked, I build out everything around it — typography pairings, colour systems, brand patterns, and mockups. This is where a logo becomes a brand identity.

Typography system — primary and secondary typeface pairing
Colour palette — all codes (HEX, RGB, CMYK), usage rules
Brand patterns and graphic elements (Full Identity package)
Photorealistic mockups showing the identity in real-world contexts
⏱ Timeline: Days 18–22
06
Delivery

Everything packaged. Ready to use.

The final delivery is a complete, organised package — not a chaotic zip file full of unnamed files. Every asset is labelled, structured, and ready to hand off to printers, developers, or your team.

Shared folder with clear folder structure — /Logo /Typography /Colours /Mockups
Every file named consistently — no 'Final_v3_FINAL2.png'
Usage guide included — so any future designer knows how to use the identity
30-day post-delivery support — for any small questions or file adjustments
⏱ Timeline: Day 23–25 (total: ~3–4 weeks)
